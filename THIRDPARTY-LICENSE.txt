Third-party assets bundled or depicted in this repository
=========================================================

Kodee (Kotlin mascot)
---------------------
The Kodee model depicted in this project's documentation/screenshots
(docs/images/) is an adaptation of Kodee, the Kotlin mascot.

    "Kodee by JetBrains s.r.o. is licensed under CC BY 4.0."
    https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by/4.0/

Changes were made: the figure was traced from the official Kodee SVG and
extruded into a 3D relief, then rendered in Konstructor. The model source is
distributed as a sample in the kcsg repository
(https://github.com/Monkopedia/KCSG).
